========================
Conventions and Examples
========================

The core of this project. Below and on the left are contventions proposed as part of this work, as well as examples of common
errors that often create confusion when discussing htis topic.

The conventions are intended to be read sequentially, and begin by redefining the concepts of **Vectors**, **Transforms**, and **Symmetry** before
building up to the more interesting topics such as  misorientations and disorientations.
